Tel Aviv

The Start-Up Nation, Silicon Wadi, Night Life

From Israel with Love

Israel is a tech-lovers' paradise! Dubbed as the 'start-up nation', Israel produces more start-ups per capita than any other country in the world! Additionally, Israel is home to beautiful beaches and scenery, a vibrant, diverse culture and amazing food. At night, go to Tel Aviv or Jerusalem to explore the thriving Israeli nightlife. Here are a few key places for a tech-traveller to visit in Israel:

Silicon Wadi

Silicon Wadi is the Silicon Valley of Israel. Israel as a nation is considered a tech cluster due to its small size. As such, there is no specific place for Silicon Wadi - it spans across areas such as Haifa, Beer Sheba, Tel Aviv, Jerusalem, Ramat Gan and many more!

Elat

Elat sits on the shores of the Red Sea with beautiful red mountains and a vibrant marine life. Visit here for watersports, snorkeling, fishing or just relaxing on the white sands in the glorious sunshine!

Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is the hub of Israel, with an unrivalled nightlife, beautiful beaches and amazing restaurants. Here you can also find the offices for the AI company Cortica, a revolutionary AI built on neuroscienctific principles.

Jerusalem

Jerusalem is known for its rich historical and cultural heritage, but it actually has a very vibrant and lively nightlife! It also is home to several tech companies, and the author of Homo Sapiens, Yuval Noah Harari!
