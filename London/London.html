Silicon Roundabout, Kings Cross, The City

London Calling!

London, the financial hub of the world, is now emerging as a major technology hub. Hosting big tech events such as CognitionX, and home to tech-integrated places such as Kings Cross, London is a tech-traveller's paradise! Here are a few things we think are worth checking out:

Silicon Roundabout

Located near the once-edgy Shoreditch, the Silicon Roundabout is the UK's answer to Silicon Valley. Thousands of tech entrepreneurs, leaders and students are to be found here, and for good reason. The area is home to giants such as Cisco, Amazon and Qualcomm.

Kings Cross

After a major regeneration push Kings Cross is now one of the safest, cleanest and architecturally stunning places in London. Home to Google UK HQ, and soon to be home to a new Facebook building, the area is attracting a wealth of tech companies and entrepreneurs. Be sure to visit on a Wednesday, Thursday or Friday to sample the amazing street food from Kerb KX!

Restaurants

All that tech travelling can really work up an appetite! Not to worry, London has you covered with some of the best restaurants in the world! Try out Chotto Matte, the Coal Office or Sushi Samba for dinner with a view!

Shopping

Shopaholic? No problem. Check out Selfridges, Harrods or Harvey Nichols if you're looking for luxury. Selfridges and Harrods both house extensive technology floors, so you can get your tech fix while you shop. Alternatively, take a trip to Brick Lane to bag a bargain!
