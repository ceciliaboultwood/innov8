Seoul

Samsung HQ, The Robot Museum, Foodie Heaven

Set your eyes on Seoul!

The home of big tech names such as Samsung, LG and Hyundai, Seoul is a thriving tech city with some of the most innovative minds on the planet. A leader in robotics, Seoul also boasts mouth-watering foodie hot spots, a beautiful skyline and a great start-up environment. Check out some of the things we love about Seoul:

Samsung HQ

Originating in Seoul, Samsung gives back to its tech community through its digital Samsung centre, a place for students and entrepreneurs to learn, network and share ideas!

Robot Museum

Seoul's robot museum is building itself - literally! The museum is due to be completed in 2022, and will be home to a collection of robots and robotic technology for the public to enjoy!

Foodie Heaven

Seoul-style chicken wings, bulgogi, hoeddeok, japchae and of course, kimchi! Seoul is a foodie hot spot for a reason, so be sure to check out all the amazing street food the city has to offer!
