Silicon Alley

Known as Europe's Silicon Valley, Silicon Allee is home to thousands of start-ups, entrpreneurs and tech students. Loved for its relatively lower cost of living, the area is attracting many tech workers from around the globe. It has an ecosystem value of $32.7 billion and its still growing.

Game Science Centre

Come visit the Game Science Centre, a small interactive museum in the heart of Berlin exhibiting an array of innovative technologies and projects. Here you can try out controlling games with your eyes or hand gestures - pretty cool stuff, eh!

Disrupt Berlin

Nightlife

Berlin is home to legendary nightclubs such as TRADE, Cocktail D'Amore, and Sunday Club. The ultimate techno scene, Berlin will surely satisfy all your clubbing needs! An added bonus - the night club scene is known for its inclusivity and and friendliness. What more could you want?
